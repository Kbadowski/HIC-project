Layered Rainbow Jello

 #1 in Trending

Looking for the perfect finger food for parties? Layered rainbow jello is a total crowd-pleaser and it’s fun and easy to make. To get into the Christmas spirit, alternate layers of lime jello with cherry, raspberry, or strawberry jello, and maybe even a layer of pink watermelon jello.
